Security Tips

Use Unique Passwords

Never reuse passwords across different websites or applications.

Mix Character Types

Combine uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols for maximum security.

Avoid Common Patterns

Don't use sequential numbers, birthdays, or predictable patterns.

Keep It Secret

Never share your passwords with anyone, including company employees.

Update Regularly

Change your passwords periodically, especially for critical accounts.

Use Password Manager

Store passwords securely in a password manager application.
